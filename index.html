GameDrobe

Automated gaming shorts publisher.

GameDrobe is an internal content-automation service that builds short-form gaming videos (game discovery, news, and highlight clips) and publishes them to the owner's own connected social accounts (TikTok, YouTube Shorts).

What it does

  • Discovers new and trending games across Steam and the web.
  • Generates short scripted videos with voice-over, captions, and b-roll.
  • Uploads finished videos to the owner's linked TikTok and YouTube accounts.

How it uses TikTok

GameDrobe uses the TikTok Content Posting API under OAuth2 credentials granted by the account owner. It only posts to the single, explicitly connected account. No messaging, no reading of other users' data.
