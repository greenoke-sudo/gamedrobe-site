GameDrobe Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Who We Are

GameDrobe is an automated gaming content publishing tool operated by the developer reachable at faruk.deniz.sapmaz@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

We only collect what's required to operate the Service:

  • OAuth access tokens for the platforms you connect (e.g., TikTok, YouTube). Tokens are stored encrypted on the server and used only to upload content you generate through the Service.
  • Content metadata you create (video titles, descriptions, tags, schedules, upload logs).
  • Basic operational logs (timestamps, error messages) for debugging and reliability.

We do not collect your platform passwords. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To publish content to the platforms you have explicitly connected.
  • To maintain and improve the reliability of the Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

When you connect a platform, that platform's own privacy policy also applies. The Service communicates with:

  • TikTok Content Posting API (for video uploads)
  • YouTube Data API (for video uploads)

5. Data Retention

Tokens and metadata are retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us. Revoking access inside the connected platform immediately invalidates our token.

6. Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards (encryption at rest, HTTPS in transit). No system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing faruk.deniz.sapmaz@gmail.com.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
